Season 1

Episodes

E1. Kassa

42 mins

Five years before the Battle of Yavin, Cassian Andor travels to the industrial planet of Morlana One on the trail of his missing sister.

E2. Reckoning

38 mins

An altercation between the trio shortly ensues outside the brothel, leading to Cassian accidentally killing one officer and executing the other.

E3. A Distant Echo

54 mins

Rael arrives on Ferrix, where he is informed by Bix as to Cassian's arrest warrant. Rael remains undeterred, and expresses particular interest in meeting Cassian in person.

E4. Shattered

40 mins

Timm, reports Cassian to Pre-Mor Security, who issue a warrant for his arrest. Karn partners with Mosk, an equally ambitious and aggressive Pre-Mor officer, to arrest Cassian.

E5. Together Again

49 mins

In a flashback, a younger Cassian, known as Kassa, and his tribe on Kenari decide to investigate a crashed ship, only to stumble upon an immense Republic mining operation.

E6. Fugitive

44 mins

Cassian flees to the planet Ferrix, where he attempts to hide his involvement by convincing his adopted mother Maarva's droid, B2EMO, and his friend, Brasso, to cover for him.

E7. Gone with a Trace

49 mins

Back on Morlana One, the chief inspector of security elects to cover up the murders, to improve his report to the Imperial authorities, but his deputy, the ambitious Syril Karn, is determined to solve the case.

E8. Downfall

41 mins

Karn identifies Cassian's borrowed ship and traces it to Ferrix, and learns from an eyewitness from the brothel that Cassian stated he was from the planet Kenari.

E9. Old Friends

51 mins

Cassian asks his friend Bix to connect him with a black market buyer, as Cassian has acquired a Starpath Unit, a valuable piece of Imperial navigation technology.

E10. Victory and Death

58 mins

Despite Cassian's desire to quickly sell the Starpath Unit and leave Ferrix, Rael tries to persuade him to join the Rebel Alliance, citing Cassian's repeated success at stealing from and sabotaging Imperial ships.

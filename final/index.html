CUNY TV Wins 21st New York Emmy Award For Animated Opening Sequence Of ‘Shades Of U.S.’

April 27, 2020

CUNY TV Art Director Adam Finger prevailed in the category of Graphic Arts: Animation for his soulful opening sequence of “Shades of U.S.,” a series that explores the complexities of people’s lives through the prisms of color, race, religion and sexual orientation. Finger’s 30-second animated show opening shines a spotlight on society’s unsung ordinary heroes through statue-like images of culturally diverse individuals engaged in everyday rituals. City Tech picked up an Emmy in the category of Education/Schools: Program Special for “Imported from Brooklyn,” a film that tells the story of renowned typography and graphic designer Tony Di Spigna, a City Tech graduate.

October 28, 2019

Another aim of this initiative is to encourage traditionally underrepresented groups to take up tech-focused areas of study.
